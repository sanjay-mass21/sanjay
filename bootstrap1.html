Vettaikkaran" refers to two different Tamil films: a 1964 classic starring M.G.R. and a 2009 action film starring Vijay. The 1964 "Vettaikaran" is about a hunter who rescues and falls in love with a lost girl. The 2009 "Vettaikkaran" centers on a young man who becomes a vigilante to fight a corrupt politician.
